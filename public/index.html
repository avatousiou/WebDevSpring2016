This is the website for Anthony Vatousiou's CS4550 Web Development class at Northeastern University.

I am currently earning my Bachelor's Degree in Computer Science with a minor in Mathematics.

The first app listed on this page is a practice app to help get the class used to using the MEAN stack. It allows users to create, update, and respond to forms.

The second application is my final project for this class. It is a website which allows users to simulate the experience of playing a pokemon game with their friends or with people anywhere in the world. Users can join leagues to battle gym leaders, collect badges, and eventually, try to take down the Elite Four of that league.
