Welcome!

Hello there! Welcome to the world of Pokemon! This website is designed to let players from across the world emulate the feeling of going out on their very own adventures and challenging the Pokemon League. Once you have registered, you will be able to build a team of Pokemon representing what you currently have in your cartridge game. With a team, you can join Leagues! Just like in the games, you will challenge eight gym leaders for their badges, and upon defeating them all, you win the right to challenge the Elite Four. Beat all four members and you receive a certificate acknowledging your prowess!
